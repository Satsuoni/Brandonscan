Terez27 Tue Dec 13
@BrandSanderson RJ did it in a pretty limited way, though. That’s why I defended it at first, but IMO it was a little gratuitous in TOM.

PeterAhlstrom Tue Dec 13
@Terez27 What do you mean, limited? Pretty much every name Jordan used was adapted or straight lifted from our modern world or mythology.

Terez27 Tue Dec 13
@PeterAhlstrom @PeterAhlstrom I was specifically talking about fan names. Even fan names don’t bother me so long as they’re smooth as the parallels all are, but in TOM, there were names dropped in when it seemed there was no practical purpose to name the characters in question. Like, the characters showed up, got named, went away, and never came back. For example “Two Maidens–Surial and Lerian–guarded the open doorway to the hallway…” and another example “They stepped through the gateway into a small alley, guarded by two Maidens–Lerian and Heidia…” In cases like that it seems like name-dropping for the sake of it. Though I’m sure the effect is exacerbated for those in the know.

PeterAhlstrom Tue Dec 13
@Terez27 RJ named a ton of characters with no practical purpose who never showed up again. Brandon’s intent was to follow that practice.

BrandSanderson Tue Dec 13
@Terez27 @PeterAhlstrom Note that in many of these cases, Harriet said to me “What are their names?” when I just wrote “Two Maidens.”

BrandSanderson Tue Dec 13
@Terez27 @PeterAhlstrom Harriet always pushes me to be more specific. You’ll find it in TGS too, where I wasn’t using fan names.

Terez27 Tue Dec 13
@BrandSanderson @PeterAhlstrom Yeah, I understand that much–and sometimes, it works. I imagine that not all examples of Harriet asking for such ended up with examples like I gave, where the names are thrown in parenthetically. In the fan criticism I mentioned earlier, these cases were always the ones fans noticed. Even at DM where the subject comes up every two weeks in a new thread, mentioned by different new users.

BrandSanderson Tue Dec 13
@Terez27 @PeterAhlstrom The examples you gave–though I only looked briefly–could have certainly been done better.

BrandSanderson Tue Dec 13
@Terez27 @PeterAhlstrom Probably, they should have just been “*** and *** went and did such and such” rather than the parenthetical.

Terez27 Tue Dec 13
@BrandSanderson @PeterAhlstrom Right, something along those lines. And again, those of us ‘in the know’ were probably bothered more by it.

BrandSanderson Tue Dec 13
@Terez27 @PeterAhlstrom We’ll try to keep an eye on the issue. We should have more time on AMoL than we did on TofM for polishing.

BrandSanderson Tue Dec 13
@Terez27 @PeterAhlstrom I don’t *Think* we’ll have a ‘shoehorn in fan names’ issue, since I have just left placeholders f
or names I need.

BrandSanderson Tue Dec 13
@Terez27 @PeterAhlstrom Every one of them is a name I’d have had to make up, but instead, I’ll adapt a fan name.

BrandSanderson Tue Dec 13
@Terez27 @PeterAhlstrom That’s more of a prose issue than anything else, though–an area where RJ was very strong and I’m relatively weak.

Terez27 Tue Dec 13
@BrandSanderson @PeterAhlstrom Yes, I’ve noticed you have mentioned that before. Which is why I thought it worth a tweet.

BrandSanderson Tue Dec 13
@Terez27 @PeterAhlstrom Still, it’s worth a look-over specifically for this issue.

JamesLuckman Wed Dec 14
@BrandSanderson @Terez27 @PeterAhlstrom I’m somewhat more troubled by the treatment of fan criticism. This is about the fifth time I’ve seen you do this Peter, with the same line “it’s a problem in reader appreciation, not the writing” and not everyone has Terez’s tenacity or confidence, and thus allow themselves to be silenced. And given I’ve seen you both cite the value of the lack of fan criticism in judging Brandon’s efforts as validation for your work on the wheel. It’s a circle, the lack of criticism held up as proof whilst the act of criticism is met with sweeping dismissal that most would never argue.

BrandSanderson Wed Dec 14
@JamesLuckman @Terez27 @PeterAhlstrom Peter is in a tough spot. I can easily say, “Yeah, I did a poor job on that one. I’ll watch for it.”

BrandSanderson Wed Dec 14

BrandSanderson Wed Dec 14
@JamesLuckman @Terez27 @PeterAhlstrom Instead, he seeks to defend me, since he knows I won’t see most people’s posts or be able to respond.

BrandSanderson Wed Dec 14

BrandSanderson Wed Dec 14
