iBooks Thu Dec 18
.@BrandSanderson Great news about Book 6 in the Mistborn Series. How long has this been in the works? link

BrandSanderson Thu Dec 18
@iBooks So, there’s a story to this. You might want to sit back for a bit and listen, because this will probably take a number of tweets…

BrandSanderson Thu Dec 18
@iBooks It all started because I knew I wanted to do Shadows of Self, the second Wax and Wayne book, and fifth Mistborn book overall.

BrandSanderson Thu Dec 18
@iBooks I’d begun working on it back during the writing of the last Wheel of Time book, between drafts. When I came back to it this year…

BrandSanderson Thu Dec 18
@iBooks …getting into it again was tough. I always have trouble reconnecting with a book when I’ve done part of it, but not finished.

BrandSanderson Thu Dec 18
.@iBooks So I worked on the outline for the Wax and Wayne sequence to get myself back into the world. In doing so, I got excited about #6.

BrandSanderson Thu Dec 18
.@iBooks So I started working on that one, and wrote the whole thing as a kind of “Warm up.” Once done, I was back into the world.

BrandSanderson Thu Dec 18
.@iBooks That made it easy to jump back to book five and finish off that one, which I was now very excited about doing.

BrandSanderson Thu Dec 18
.@iBooks So…yeah. I kind of wrote a book as a warm up to write a different book. My process can be crazy sometimes, but it really worked.
