ericpeters Mon Nov 14
@BrandSanderson What exactly is a dueling cane anyhow? #torchat

BrandSanderson Mon Nov 14
@ericpeters Depends. Some are flexible, like sparring swords, and are used for duels where blood is not needed. #torchat

BrandSanderson Mon Nov 14
@ericpeters Others are basically a big length of wood for hitting people, like a tonfa without the grip. #torchat

ericpeters Mon Nov 14
@BrandSanderson Do canes have a hilt like a sparring sword then? I always picture something like pic being used #torchat

BrandSanderson Mon Nov 14
@ericpeters Many have a hilt. However for most, there is no crossguard or the like. #torchat

BrandSanderson Mon Nov 14
@ericpeters And most don’t taper like that one. They are often of a uniform diameter all the way down. #torchat

BenjaminSRawlin Mon Nov 14
@BrandSanderson how old were you when you first started writing? any advice for young writers, given economy at the moment? #torchat

BrandSanderson Mon Nov 14
@BenjaminSRawlin I started at age 15 or 16, but didn’t finish a novel until in my 20s. As for new writers, don’t worry about the money.

BrandSanderson Mon Nov 14
@BenjaminSRawlin It will probably take a few years to get published, or to be making money, so focus on the craft first. #torchat

BrandSanderson Mon Nov 14
@BenjaminSRawlin Think of becoming a writer like becoming a doctor. It can take 6-8 yrs. Who knows what the economy will be like? #torchat

BrandSanderson Mon Nov 14
@RachelxRussell This usually makes my sf shorter than my fantasy. #torchat

BrandSanderson Tue Nov 15
@vetemix So when the book uses a word like those, the translator from the original Mistborn language feels it is the closest approximation.
