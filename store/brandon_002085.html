OracleAlchemist Mon Oct 17
@BrandSanderson I’ve got 20 character sheets, a bunch of maps, a full chapter outline, and 50 pages down and I’m STUCK. WTH’s wrong w/ me!

BrandSanderson Tue Oct 18
@OracleAlchemist Ha. Well, it’s hard to say specifically. It could be that you’re secretly a discovery writer.

BrandSanderson Tue Oct 18
@OracleAlchemist More likely, you just need more stuff to happen. Go to your outline, make a list of things that need to happen in the book.

BrandSanderson Tue Oct 18
@OracleAlchemist From there, break each event to small steps. Try to identify twenty big things that happen in the book, and their pieces.

BrandSanderson Tue Oct 18
@OracleAlchemist Then, take three of those pieces, add an interesting location, and write the next chapter to achieve those 3 goals.
