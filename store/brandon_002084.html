@BrandSanderson I seek advice,please how do you write humor into a character if your not necessarily funny yourself?

BrandSanderson Wed Oct 05
@Ohh_whaaat For witty humor—you can be funny, as you get to think about your replies out ahead of time. Pitch perfect lines to characters.

BrandSanderson Wed Oct 05
@Ohh_whaaat For character humor, put people into situations outside of their comfort zone and have them muddle about.

BrandSanderson Wed Oct 05
@Ohh_whaaat Someone like Mat is tough because his humor is a mixture of untrustworthy narrator and outrageous thought patterns.

BrandSanderson Wed Oct 05
